## Review Standards

### Red Lines (Must Fix)
- Content damaging national image or dignity
- Detailed torture/murder descriptions
- Explicit sexual content
- Inappropriate content involving minors
- Glorifying crime without consequences
- Distorting historical facts

### Yellow Lines (Recommend Fix)
- Excessive violence (use camera cuts instead)
- Excessive romantic content (use implication instead)
- Protagonist above the law (use legal means)
- Money solves everything (add wisdom/courage elements)
- Female objectification (give independent personality)

### Green (Pass)
- Moderate conflict and confrontation
- Reasonable emotional expression
- Positive values conveyed
- Professional format compliance

### Overseas Additional Checks (when target market includes overseas)
- Religious content neutrality
- Racial/ethnic equality
- Violence rating compliance (TV-14 / TV-MA)
- Platform policy compliance (ReelShort / DramaBox)

## Character Design Principles

1. Total main + supporting characters no more than 5-20
2. Every character must have clear desires and conflicts
3. Villains in four tiers: Fodder -> Mid-boss -> Final Boss -> Hidden Villain
4. Character arcs must show clear transformation
5. Catchphrases should be distinctive
6. Hidden villain must have earlier foreshadowing support

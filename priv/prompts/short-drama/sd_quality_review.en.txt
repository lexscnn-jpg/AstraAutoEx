## Scoring Standards

| Score Range | Grade | Recommendation |
|------------|-------|----------------|
| 45-50 | Excellent | Ready to use |
| 35-44 | Good | Minor adjustments needed |
| 25-34 | Passing | Revisions needed |
| Below 25 | Failing | Rewrite recommended |

## Assessment Principles

1. Rhythm: Does the opening enter conflict within 30 seconds? Any dragging sections?
2. Satisfaction: Count meets target (2-3)? Intensity sufficient? Types varied?
3. Dialogue: Colloquial? Character distinction? Any filler?
4. Format: Scene headers complete? Shot types annotated? Music cues present?
5. Continuity: Any contradictions with known context? Character behavior consistent?

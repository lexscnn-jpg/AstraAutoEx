## Outline Principles

1. Act 1 (15% of episodes): Quick setup, first 3 episodes must establish main conflict
2. Act 2 (65% of episodes): Continuous escalation, mini-climax every 5-8 episodes, major twist at midpoint
3. Act 3 (20% of episodes): Full resolution, all foreshadowing paid off
4. Rhythm curve: No 3 consecutive high-intensity episodes, no 2 consecutive low-intensity episodes
5. Significant climax every 5 episodes, explosive climax every 15-20 episodes
6. Paywalls must be placed at the end of the most emotionally intense episodes
